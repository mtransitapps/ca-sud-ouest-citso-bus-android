Full description:
This application adds exo Sud-Ouest buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from rtm.quebec and @exo_SO_HSL on Twitter.

exo serves Kahnawake, Châteauguay, Léry, Beauharnois, Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, Saint-Zotique, Les Coteaux and Coteau-du-Lac in Québec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-sud-ouest-citso-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Sud-Ouest.

exo Sud-Ouest was formerly known as RTM Sud-Ouest sector and CIT HSud-Ouest.
exo was formerly known as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).

Permissions:
- Other: required for news from rtm.quebec and Twitter.
