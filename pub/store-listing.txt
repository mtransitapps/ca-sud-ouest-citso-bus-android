Full description:
This application adds CIT Sud-Ouest buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from citso.org and @CitsoInfo on Twitter.

CIT Sud-Ouest serves Kahnawake, Châteauguay, Léry, Beauharnois, Salaberry-de-Valleyfield, Saint-Zotique, Les Coteaux and Coteau-du-Lac.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport.
https://www.amt.qc.ca/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-sud-ouest-citso-bus-android

This app is not related with the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport and CIT Sud-Ouest.

Permissions:
- Other: required for news from citso.org and Twitter.
